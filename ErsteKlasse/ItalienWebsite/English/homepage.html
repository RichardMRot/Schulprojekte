Italy

A beautiful country, which is located in the center south of Europe and serves as the home of 60 million people.
It is a peninsula and is mostly surrounded by the Mediterranean Sea, and the Alps in the north. It is also home to the Apennine mountain range.
Italy is a parliamentary republic and is split in 20 (political) regions.
The national languages is italian, but there are many other dialects.
This country is also the fifth largest travel destination.
It is especially known for its beaches and food.
