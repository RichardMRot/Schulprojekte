How to use this?

The Stripe of Fifths is like the Circle of Fifths, except, well, it is in the form of two Stripes. The left stripe adds a flat note every scale and the right stripe adds a sharp note every scale. The outer scales are major, the inner ones are minor.

Click on one of the letters (scales) and the chords that belong to it will get shown.
