How to use this?

Did you find a chord that you don't know how to play? Maybe you don't understand a symbol or it's just complicated to find out what it's supposed to be like.

Anyways, just put it in that input-field there and then press the button down below. Then you'll see the notes you need for it on a piano keyboard. You can also hear what it sounds like.
