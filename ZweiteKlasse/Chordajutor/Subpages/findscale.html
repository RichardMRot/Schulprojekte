Find Scale

How to use this?

You put the (text with) chords in the upper box and then click the "Find Scale" button, which will then find the scale. Make sure that, if your text contains lyrics, the chords and the lyrics are separated into different lines. Also, if you have more than one scale (not necessarily counting borrowed chords) it might not work, though.
